Chico Xavier

Here's a time line of Chico Xavier life:

  • April 2, 1910 - Born in Pedro Leopoldo, Minas Gerais
  • 1932 - Publication of Chico Xavier's first book with 256 poems.
  • 1959 - Chico Xavier moved to Uberaba, where he resided.
  • 1975 - Foundation of the Spiritist Group of Prayer by Chico Xavier.
  • June 30, 2002 - Chico Xavier passed away in Uberaba, Minas Gerais..

"God gives us each day a page of life in the book of time. What we put on it is up to us."

-- Chico Xavier
